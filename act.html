THE TECHNICAL SKILLS DEVELOPMENT CENTRE

• The Centre aims at providing the students with hands-on skills and training in Glasswork, Metalwork and Woodwork.
• TheCentredisplaysafinesymbiosisoftraditionaland modern techniques that are used by the students to design and create artefacts in wood, metal, M-Seal, fibre, plastic, glass, etc. The students are also trained in operating on 3D printer, 3D scanner and CNC Router to make exclusive products.
• Students learn to design and manufacture trophies for some school events.
• They gain equal expertise in using machines and hand tools like lathe, planer, arc welding, gas welding, jigsaw, drill, different types of saws, grinders, etc. to design and create original artwork in various mediums.
• Students are also trained in traditional stained-glasswork (Leaded and with Copper foil method) and operation of the glass furnace where they make products through processes like fusing, slumping, casting etc.
• The Robotics lab is housed at the centre.

ART SCHOOL

An exemplary program for Art where students learn sketching, painting, sculpture making, pottery, photography, screen printing, clay modelling, ceramics, wood carving, carpet weaving, recycled paper making and art conservation and development. The campus, with its heritage buildings, colorful flora and fauna provides inspiration to the young artists.

MUSIC SCHOOL

Music is an integral part of education at Mayo College and every student is exposed to a wide array of musical instruments – both Western and Indian. The school has an impressive Department of Music with experts in every field from Tabla and Sitar to Violin and Ukulele. More than 50 types of instruments are taught to the students at the Music School! The school has its own Brass Band, a Western Band named 'Arpeggios' and a School Choir. The school is an examination centre for the Trinity College of London for Music.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

We focus on 'Thinking globally on issues of society and acting locally to bring about change in ourselves and our communities'. Community service is conducted at three levels - within the school, in and around the city and at the global level by participating in international service projects. Every student engages in Community Service through a plethora of projects which help inculcate social responsibility, dignity of labor and cultural sensitivity.
