About the School

Mayo College is a unique blend of modern education being delivered in a historical setting to raise global citizens, who are capable of taking on the future, yet conscious and proud of their rich cultural legacy.

DIRECTOR’S MESSAGE

Mayo College has created a niche for itself as a premier residential educational institution for boys. The school provides an ideal platform to ingrain a deep-rooted value system in our students. At Mayo College, schooling is a perfect blend of academic excellence, technological skills, sports, fine arts, music and theatre. It is a unique place where cultural legacy happily coexists with technological advancements. We are deeply committed to imparting holistic education which has intrinsic value, apart from its function of increasing vocational opportunities. Our responsibility lies not only in the development of academic skills, but also vital non-cognitive skills. The team at Mayo College comprises of well-qualified teachers and instructors supported by experienced administrative staff. The curriculum is constantly evolving to meet the needs of a changing world. It promotes academic excellence along with qualities of leadership, self-confidence, mutual respect and a sense of global identity. Being a residential school, we take adequate measures to ensure quality pastoral care for our students. We look forward to being equal partners in your child's growth and education. Give your child the advantage of Mayo and know why generations of Mayoites are so proud of their Alma Mater!

LT GEN S H KULKARNI
PVSM, AVSM, VSM** (Retd)

ORIGIN & LEGACY

Mayo College is a fully residential public boarding school for boys in Ajmer, Rajasthan. It was founded in 1875 by the then Viceroy of India – Richard Bourke, 6th Earl of Mayo, with contributions from the princely kingdoms of Rajputana, to provide modern education, that was Indian in essence,tothearistocracy.Afterindependence,theschooladopted a secular and egalitarian philosophy and opened its doors to students from all communities and regions across the nation and the world.

Why Mayo?

Because at Mayo every studen is offered the perfect blend of Academics, Co-curriculars and Sports.
• Teacher-Student ratio 1:10, emphasis on Academics, six languages offered, 15 subjects from Science, Commerce and Humanities streams with outstanding results in Board Examinations.
• All 750 boys can play more than 20 sports simultaneously under the supervision of resident coaches on the extensive facilities present on the campus.
• 20+ distinct Co-curricular programs for all round development of Students.

The Mayo Advantage

• An Outdoor School where students are encouraged to spend time in the open in the lap of nature overlooked by majestic Aravali hills.
• Emphasis on developing Well Rounded Personalities that are capable of adapting to and taking on the challenges of the changing world unlike ‘mass produced’ single faceted individuals churned out of the more conventional schools.
• In consonance with the concept of an ‘Outdoor School’ Mayo College looks at its Campus as a Classroom and believes that learning happens both inside and outside classrooms.
• Each student an Entity - not a batch statistic. We aim to nurture individuality in a community environment.
• A Separate Junior School for class 4 – 6 to allow our younger students to discover their talents and flourish in a friendly environment suitable to their age.

Unique Feartues of Mayo

Mayo College has a lush green 187 acres campus, with a beautiful view of the Aravali hills.

• SELF SUFFICIENT CAMPUS Having its own hospital, temple, post office, bank, barber shop, a café, store for basic provisions, guest house, museum, stables, golf course and even a lake!
• A CASHLESS CAMPUS Nowhere on the campus are students required or permitted to use cash in any form.
• A SAFE CAMPUS Campus security is managed by a competent team of guards supervised by a professional Security Officer.
Entry into and out of the campus is strictly regulated and monitored. The boundary walls are well maintained and closely guarded by security cameras so as to ensure the safety and security of students at all times. The school complies with all government rules and regulations relevant to a school of its kind.
• A SMART CAMPUS The School has modern Information Communication Technology facilities. The whole campus is centrally networked. Intranet and Internet facilities are widely available with supervision to enable students and staff to access educational & multimedia content from anywhere on campus
