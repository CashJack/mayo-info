TRAVEL DETAILS

As an additional help to the parents, the College arranges as best convenient for it, the boys' travel to the College at the beginning of the term, and from the College at the end of the term. Usually escorted parties are taken to Delhi, Mumbai, Howrah, Lucknow, Ahmedabad, Patna, Guwahati, Amritsar, Jammu and Allahabad. It must be made clear that these arrangements are undertaken by the College as a voluntary assistance to the parents and not as an obligatory duty. Although all normal precautions are taken, the College or individual members of staff will not be responsible for any inconvenience, loss or damage on these trips. Travel arrangements by air can also be made for Mumbai, Calcutta, Bangalore and Guwahati. Requests for such arrangements must be received at the beginning of the term.

TRAVELLING (SCHOOL TO HOME / HOME TO SCHOOL)

All the boys travelling in organized School Parties escorted by Teachers, from School to home or from home to School, must observe the following rules:

  • They will travel in School Uniform. They will be responsible for their own luggage.
  • Boys must follow the instructions of the Teacher-in-charge of the Party.
  • No boy should detrain, get down from the bus without the permission of his escort.
  • No boy should leave the station or meander away from the party without the permission of the escorts. They must not walk away with their parents/guardians without the permission of the Escorts.
  • Boys should not carry heavy luggage. They should travel light.
