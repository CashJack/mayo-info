CARRER COUNSELLING/OUTREACH CENTRE

Is the newly developed Outreach Centre. As the interface between the chool and the community, it houses Round Square Internation Senior and Junior Programs, Model United Nations Program, IPSC, IAYP, Faculty development program. The school Debating Society and The School Alumni Society. The Outreach Centere ata Mayo College aims to make the education our students receive relvant Beyond Curriculum, Beyond Campus and Beyond Class XII. By housing myriad activities that facilitate our students’ and faculty’s interaction with the world outside, the centre in a way bring the world into the Mayo Campus.
What afer class XII
Mayo college has a dedicated career guidance counselor who manages and runs a comprehensive career guidance program. Every student in senior school is provided customized one-on-one support for subject selection, identifying preferred career paths, building desired skills and competitive portfolios and selecting 'good fit colleges. Regular lectures by experts, psychometric tests, university meets, career visits and college fairs are held. The Mayo career fair is attended by a host of international and national universities. The centre also runs an internship program for students. The school has a customized career guidance platform to which every student from grade IX onwards has access. Every year a large number of our students are placed in world renowned universities in India and abroad. The School holds preparatory classes for CLAT, PSAT/SAT and is also a center for PSAT, SAT and Trinity College, London, Speech, Drama and Music exams.
COUNSELLING CENTRE
The school has a full-time counselor to provide psychological counseling and emotional support to students. She is available twenty-four-seven for the students in their times of need. Life skills are being taught as a part of the school curriculum to help students develop psychological, social and emotional maturity. Staff, involved with the students as foster parents, are given special training to handle the challenges that students experience. Empathy Boxes are placed all over the school to allow students to seek an appointment with the counselor. Students can also visit her office if required.

CAREER FEST

Effective learning also requires great teachers. Mayo College takes pride in its experienced and extremely competent faculty. A lot of emphasis is laid on Faculty Development. Besides regular In-House training, our faculty regularly participates in workshops, seminars and orientation programmes in India and abroad, conducted by CBSE, IPSC, IIT and IIM. Every year the school invites leading experts to deliver lectures and training sessions for our staff with the aims of ensuring that they remain updated with the latest trends in education and that their Soft Skills are constantly enhanced.

PREPARE FOR NEET/JE/CAT/NDA

We have an Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Robotics Lab on campus where students are taught how to make robots from scratch and then to program them using C++ and Python.
The school is also equipped with a 3 D scanner and printer. Students are taught how to feed models into the computer to create three dimensional solid objects. 3 D printing (also called additive manufacturing) is already being used to make everything from homes to aircraft and its use is likely to expand in the coming years.
